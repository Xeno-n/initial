List of 9 Planets

In our solar system, planets are categorized into two main groups: terrestrial (rocky) and gas giants. Here’s a list of the planets:

  • Mercury: The closest planet to the Sun, known for its extreme temperature fluctuations.
  • Venus: Earth's "sister planet," known for its thick, toxic atmosphere and extreme heat.
  • Earth: The only known planet to support life, with a diverse range of habitats.
  • Mars: Known as the Red Planet, has the highest mountain and the largest canyon in the solar system.
  • Jupiter: The largest planet, recognized for its Great Red Spot and many moons.
  • Saturn: Famous for its stunning rings made of ice and rock particles.
  • Uranus: An ice giant characterized by its unique blue color and tilted axis.
  • Neptune: Known for its deep blue color and strong winds, the farthest planet from the Sun.
  • Pluto: Once considered the ninth planet, now classified as a dwarf planet.
